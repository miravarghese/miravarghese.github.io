Blueberry-Papaya Smoothie Bowl

Kick off back-to-school season with this easy, healthy blueberry-papaya smoothie bowl that you'll love just as much as your kids.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh papaya, peeled, deseeded and cubed
  • 1/2 cup frozen wild blueberries
  • 1 banana, divided
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons chia seeds, divided
  • 2 teaspoons date syrup
  • 1 cup initial-scale
  • almond granola, for garnish
  • flaked coconut, for garnish

Preparation

  1. Using a kitchen blender add the papaya, frozen blueberries, 1/2 banana, almond milk, 1 teaspoon chia seeds, date syrup and ice. Blend on high for 1 minute.
  2. 2Pour mixture into two serving bowls. Top with the remaining banana, thinly sliced, chia seeds, granola and flaked coconut. Serve immediately.
